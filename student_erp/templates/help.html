📘 How to Use Student ERP

1. Apply for ID Card from the ID Card section.

2. Download certificates from the Certificates section.

3. Enroll in additional courses from the Courses section.

4. Cancel courses anytime from the Enrolled Courses list.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for an ID card?
Go to Dashboard → ID Card → Apply for ID Card.

Q: How do I download a certificate?
Open Certificates section and click Download.

Q: How do I enroll in a course?
Go to Courses page and click the Enroll button.

Q: How do I cancel a course?
Go to Enrolled Courses and click Cancel.
